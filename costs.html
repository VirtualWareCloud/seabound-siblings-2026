Expedition Costs

Rowing 3,000 miles unsupported across the Atlantic is an extraordinary undertaking that carries extraordinary costs. Our estimated budget for a two-person crew is USD $180,000 – $200,000, covering everything from the race entry fee and boat, to safety gear, shipping, and contingency funds. A transparent cost breakdown is shown below so every sponsor and supporter knows exactly where funds go.

Budget ItemEstimated Cost (USD)
Race Entry Fee$25,000
Ocean Rowing Boat (new, fully equipped)$70,000
Safety & Navigation Equipment$40,000
Shipping & Logistics (UK ↔ La Gomera ↔ Antigua)$20,000
Food & Provisions (2 × 70 days @ 6,000 kcal)$10,000
Training Courses & Qualifications$5,000
Insurance & Contingency Reserve$10,000
Total Expedition Budget$180,000
